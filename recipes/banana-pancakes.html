Banana Pancakes

Description

Wake up on the right side of the bed with a stack of sweet, cozy, and simple banana pancakes.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • 1 Egg
  • Milk
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Mix. Stir until ingredients are incorporated
  2. Cook. Pour the batter in Â¼ cup portions onto a lightly oiled pan or griddle over medium-high heat. Cook for a few minutes, flip with a spatula, and cook for another few minutes
  3. Serve. Add whatever toppings you like!
